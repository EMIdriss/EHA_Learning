All About Green Nature

Green is the quintessential colour of nature - of parks, gardens and forests, and a reminder of the life-giving power of plants. For animals, it's a complicated colour to produce using pigments.
Green is the color of life, reflected in nature as a sign of renewal. As a bright, positive color, green has many cultural meanings in religion and art.
Green is much more than a color. It has come to symbolize our natural environment and the planet Earth and how important it is to safeguard them for our own benefit and that of future generations.
