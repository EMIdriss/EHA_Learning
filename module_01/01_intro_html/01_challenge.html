Never Be Stagnant In Learning

Learning makes us better people,
And gives us the power to conquer our world.

Do not let classroom education,
Makes you feel you have gotten it all;
Remember what you know in class,
Is just 30% of shared knowledge;
And the remaining 70% worth of education,
You get them through constant self-learning.

What you discover by yourself,
Is only what will live within your heart;
And this would only be made possible,
If you subject yourself to constant reading.

Deep knowledge is gotten,
When we persistently read;
Deep learning, gives us a broader view of life,
And how to deal with different kinds of people;
That would forever constantly come your way.

Self-learning is a character builder,
Anyone who seeks to be happy;
In all such would ever seek to do,
Should learn to make her a true friend.

Get all the qualifications you ever need,
But if you fail to educate yourself;

Through daily continuous learning,
Then you have forever limited yourself;
Within the confinement of limitation.
